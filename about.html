★ About Me =(^w^=

Hi! My name is Amber!

I'm a graphic designer, content creator, and fan of all things tech. I'm best known as the lead graphic designer for the Sonic Runners Revival project, or as the vtuber Kohaku Aishogeki!

I've spent a majority of my life plugged into the internet, and have experimented in the past with running a website on Google Sites. But as the web deviates from what I know and love, leaving the aging computers I'm familiar with behind, I've made it a goal and priority of mine to support decentralized, accessible software and services that don't rely on mega corporations, and to keep my computers and the sites they visit debloated.

The Jukebox is benchmarked on a 2001 iBook G4 clocked at 800MHz with 1GB of RAM, using TenFourFox, on Mac OS X 10.4.11.

This site is proudly anti-Javascript.

Thanks for taking the time to visit. If you want to get ahold of me, either regarding this site or just to say hello, you can reach me via the social icons on the right, or feel free to leave feedback and comments in the guestbook! Thank you!
