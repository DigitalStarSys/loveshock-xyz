★ Music! ★

Welcome to our music page!

With our autism, we generally can't function without music in our day-to-day life. The Jukebox was powered by music, as was our love for so many different forms of art and self-expression.

This page serves as our documenting and love letters to all things music - the songs we listen to, the devices that play them, and the stories behind them.

We've also attached Webamp to this page, and loaded it with some of our DJ sets and favorite songs. Feel free to listen while you browse~!
