The Story

Prominitheus is the portable, mini replacement for Prometheus, our old desktop gaming machine. As portability and movability became a concern, Prometheus was sold to someone else, and Prominitheus was designated as the replacement machine.

The gaming SSD from Prometheus was put inside the laptop's spare NVMe slot, and the 4TB archival drive now lives in an external USB enclosure.
